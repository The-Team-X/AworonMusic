About us!

Aworon is a Guwahati based band, it was formed in the year 2018. But, it gained its pace from the year 2019 and has been working on several originals since then. We play rock under an independent record level. Aworon has a vision of adding a different taste in the music scene and making it more diverse in taste. Aworon consists of Nairit Ishan Nath (Vocal), Anurag Mon Gogoi (Guitar), Sagar K Hazarika (Bass Guitar), Joysurya Kashyap (Guitar) and Nilutpal Nath(Drums).

"Akuwalisu" is an Assamese Rock Song, which depicts the struggles in life which everyone faces, here the protagonist is a boy who was a victim of bullying and had a tragic childhood. It revolves around the idea of getting back stronger and pushing the limits to uplift oneself. The song tries to show every one of us, that learning and growing from the struggles, is the key to enlighten our life. Fighting back to the problems and barriers we face is the Ultimate Solution.
